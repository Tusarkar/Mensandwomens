Our house

Cloth first. Noise never.

Loom & Line began as a small pattern room obsessed with drape and grain — two collections, one standard: pieces you reach for without thinking, season after season.

What we care about

  • Fit you can trust — block patterns refined over years, not trend cycles.
  • Fabric with a story — mills we know by name; weights chosen for how they age.
  • Calm shelves — fewer SKUs, clearer edits, less clutter in your wardrobe.

Questions

Do you ship across India?

Yes. Standard and express options are available at checkout on our live store. This static preview is for layout only.

How do I find my size?

Each edit includes a measurement chart. When in doubt, size up for outerwear and down for fine knits — or write us through Contact.

Returns & exchanges

Unworn items with tags may be exchanged within 14 days. Final sale on altered or hemmed pieces.
